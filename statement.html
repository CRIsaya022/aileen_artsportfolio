Artist Statement

"I feel lucky to have been introduced to the art of painting through my uncle, MacFadyne (Mac) Sawaya. Visiting his studio just upstairs in our home and being immersed in the world of tinga-tinga art—the smell of varnish, the vibrant colors, and the sound of him moving around his tools—was an experience unlike any other. It both humbled and excited me. Stepping into that space felt sacred. When I’d ask him to let me experiment with paint, he'd always say no, handing me a pencil and drawing paper, telling me to ‘play’ with those instead. Now, looking back, I take that personally in the best way.

Lots of love, Aileen."
