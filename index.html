About Me

I am a contemporary visual artist born and raised in the vibrant city of Arusha, Tanzania. Passionate about color and form, I use a variety of mediums—from acrylics to digital media—to express thoughts and emotions through art. My pieces have been showcased in galleries across Tanzania and the United States, each one telling a unique story of identity, resilience, and the captivating beauty of everyday life.
